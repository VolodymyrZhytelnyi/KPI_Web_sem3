How to cook a whole chicken?

When grilling a whole chicken, butterfly it. When the backbone is removed, the chicken can be flattened and spread more evenly across the grill to ensure all parts cook in the same amount of time.

How to use fresh herbs?

Add fresh herbs toward the end of cooking. This helps their flavor come through and prevents discoloring.
